Sweet & Salty Today

We have come a long way over the years and today we are proud to be the Canada leading supplier of sandwich bread to sandwich manufacturers. We are straight forward and our values keep us true. We take real care in providing our customers with the best bread – it’s as simple as that! Our aim is to be the best at what we do. We pride ourselves on the quality of our bread and our team; working together to ensure our customers experience is an enjoyable one.

Our Bread

What made us we make everyday. It’s our job as bakers to make it count by making it good. But we will.
